Chair Sim
Chair Simulation 🪑
Practice makes calm
Simulate your entire appointment step by step.
Step 1 of 5
Step 1 — Arrival
Walking into the clinic
You walk in. The receptionist smiles. You check in and take a seat. The waiting area has soft lighting.
Step 2 — The Chair
Sitting in the dental chair
The chair is comfortable — like a recliner. The dentist greets you warmly and tells you exactly what they'll do.
Step 3 — The Sounds
Hearing the tools
The cleaning tool makes a gentle buzzing sound. It's not as loud as you imagined. Your hand signal is ready.
Step 4 — The Procedure
The cleaning begins
Water, suction, gentle pressure. The dentist narrates every step. You're breathing steadily. It's manageable.
Step 5 — Done!
You made it!
The chair rises. "Great job today." You rinse, sit up, and feel a wave of relief and pride. That's it.

About Us

🌿 Our Mission
SerenOra Care exists to make dental visits feel safe, calm, and manageable for everyone — especially those living with dental anxiety. We believe fear should never be a barrier to good oral health.
💙 Who We Are
Built by a team passionate about mental health and dentistry, SerenOra bridges the gap between patients and their care teams — with tools for breathing, sound desensitization, cognitive reframing, and real dentist connection.
