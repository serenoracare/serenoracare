About CitRoha

The name
holds the mission.

CitRoha Pvt Ltd was founded on a conviction that every healthcare challenge is also a consciousness challenge — a gap in awareness, access, or action. We exist to close those gaps.

Derived from two Sanskrit roots, our name is not a brand exercise. It is a reminder: that every company we build must remain conscious of the lives it touches, and that growth must always mean rising toward something better.

We are India-first and patient-first. Our ventures launch here, learn here, and scale outward — carrying the rigour of Indian healthcare needs as the gold standard for everything we do.

SerenOra Care
Dental Care · India

SerenOra Care is CitRoha's flagship healthcare brand, dedicated to making quality dental care accessible, trustworthy, and compassionate for every Indian. Oral health is the gateway to whole-body wellness — a connection too often overlooked. SerenOra combines clinical excellence with patient education, closing the awareness gap one smile at a time.
